# Tile Maintenance: Keep Your Floors Looking New Proper maintenance ensures your tile floors remain beautiful for decades. Follow these expert tips: ## Daily Maintenance ### Sweeping and Vacuuming - Sweep or vacuum daily to remove dirt and debris - Use soft-bristle broom or vacuum without beater bar - Prevents scratches from abrasive particles ### Spot Cleaning - Wipe spills immediately - Use damp cloth for quick cleanup - Prevents staining ## Weekly Cleaning ### Mopping - Use warm water and pH-neutral cleaner - Avoid harsh chemicals that damage grout - Change water frequently - Dry with clean towel to prevent water spots ### Recommended Cleaners - Mild dish soap diluted in water - Commercial tile cleaners (pH-neutral) - White vinegar solution (for non-natural stone) ## Monthly Deep Cleaning ### Grout Cleaning - Use grout brush and specialized cleaner - Scrub in circular motions - Rinse thoroughly - Consider professional cleaning annually ### Sealing - Reseal grout every 1-2 years - Natural stone needs sealing 1-2 times per year - Prevents staining and moisture damage ## What to Avoid ‚ùå Acidic cleaners on natural stone ‚ùå Abrasive scrubbers that scratch ‚ùå Oil-based cleaners that leave residue ‚ùå Excessive water that seeps into grout ‚ùå Steam cleaners on certain tile types ## Professional Maintenance Consider professional cleaning: - Every 12-18 months for high-traffic areas - When grout becomes discolored - Before resealing - For stubborn stains E.S.
